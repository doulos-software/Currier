# Exegesis

## What is it

 Exegesis is gleaning from scripture

- gospel story
- human story
- sin
- decrees
- commands
- promises
  - unconditional
  - conditional
- principles
- themes


## Fallacies

### Foreign Root

The meaning of a root word does not impute meaning to the derived word.
The current context is ultimate.

### Shared Roots

timao, epitimao
honor, rebuke
